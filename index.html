Her relationships are the most important thing to her, and product management allows her work with fabulous people and teams. Product Experience

Abby has helped create five different products for EY to bring to market. These are mostly in the Banking sector, but she has also worked on core platform products too. She's proven to be a dynamic problem solver throughout the entire product lifecycle - from product inception through product delivery. She has spent time performing user testing and production ideation, architecting detailed solution plans, delivering products to market, and building the commercial pricing and delivery of a product. Below is a sampling of some of her work.

Intelligent Core

Abby is currently overseeing the design and build of an EY Nexus product called Intelligent Core. The solution is aimed at helping clients de-risk, augment, and accelerate the core banking modernization journey. Through this work, she has laid out the product roadmap, helped define a strategy to align product build to industry standards like BIAN, collaborated with FinTech and EY Alliance partners like Finxact, and worked with global teams to gather requirements, support client engagements, and build a meaningful solution.

Configuration Studio

Earlier this year, Abby led a team that built a low-code, no code solution: the Configuration Studio. This product is designed to help non-technical users create user and data experiences in an easy to use interface. The product helps democratize the EY Nexus platform and helps EY teams building on the platform access the components as well as EY Nexus clients. Abby worked in depth with the event streaming architecture to create this solution and has gained an appreciation to the types of experiences real-time technology can enable for use cases across sectors.

Intelligent Disputes

Intelligent Disputes was one of Abby's first products on the EY Nexus team. The solution helps clients tackle their disputes challenges using data enrichment, self-service tooling, intelligent processing, and disputes optimization strategies. The product has successfully supported client engagements since its inception in 2020.

Awards & Recognition

Abby has been part of the Nexus team for four years and has seen the team evolve from a few people trying to figure out how to help the banking industry to a platform with over 300 team members globally. The team has matured and crystallized its strategy to deliver industry leading solutions and Abby is proud to be part of a group that is looking to innovate how EY serves its clients. In 2023 the EY Nexus product management team was recognized with a Stevie, an industry award for new products in the market that are making an impact.
